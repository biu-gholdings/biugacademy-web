Regulatory alignment

Design standards and disclosure

All programs are being designed in accordance with the guidelines of the Ministério do Ensino Superior, Ciência, Tecnologia e Inovação (MESCTI) and the Instituto Nacional de Avaliação, Acreditação e Reconhecimento de Estudos do Ensino Superior (INAAREES).

BIU.G Academy does not currently confer accredited academic degrees. Certification programs are issued independently and are under progressive development toward formal recognition and accreditation within the Angolan higher education framework.

Why Angola first

Angola needs practical technical education that matches labour-market demand, strengthens digital infrastructure talent, and improves financial literacy at scale. BIU.G Academy prioritises Angola so that curricula, examples, and delivery assumptions reflect local institutions, languages of instruction, and connectivity realities. From that foundation, content and partnerships can extend across Africa without losing rigour or relevance.
