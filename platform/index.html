Platform

A single learning environment is under development to host courses, track progress, and support community and mentorship as capacity grows.

Capabilities in scope

Online courses

Structured modules with clear objectives, assessments where appropriate, and revision-friendly pacing.

Independent certificates

Digital attestations of completion issued by BIU.G Academy, distinct from government-accredited qualifications.

Student dashboard

Enrolments, deadlines, and resource links in one dashboard as the product matures.

Community

Moderated spaces for cohort discussion, aligned with code-of-conduct standards under development.

Mentorship

Office-hour style touchpoints as mentor pools become available per programme.

Offline-first learning

Download-friendly materials and low-bandwidth paths where licensing and security allow.

Mobile access

Responsive layouts for common phones and tablets used in Angola and the region.

Future developer ecosystem integrations

APIs and tooling hooks planned for ecosystems such as CubeShackles; nothing production-grade is implied at this stage.

Security and data

Personal data submitted through the waitlist is used only for evaluation and communication related to BIU.G Academy and related developer ecosystem opportunities, subject to the consent language you accept on the contact form. A full privacy policy will be published before public enrolment opens.
