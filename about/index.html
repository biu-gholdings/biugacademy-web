About BIU.G Academy

An Angola-first technical education and training initiative, structured for alignment with national higher-education guidance as programs mature.

What we are building

BIU.G Academy is a technical education and training initiative under active development. It is sponsored by Biu-g Holdings LLC with a focus on practical skills: financial literacy, entrepreneurship, software development, digital infrastructure, markets education, civic technology, and career readiness. Content and delivery models are being shaped for Angolan learners first, then extended across the continent where appropriate.

What we are not

We are not a university, we do not confer accredited degrees, and we do not represent partnerships with named universities for credit transfer. Any future formal recognition will be communicated transparently as it is obtained under Angolan law and INAAREES processes.

Regulatory disclosure

All programs are being designed in accordance with the guidelines of the Ministério do Ensino Superior, Ciência, Tecnologia e Inovação (MESCTI) and the Instituto Nacional de Avaliação, Acreditação e Reconhecimento de Estudos do Ensino Superior (INAAREES).

BIU.G Academy does not currently confer accredited academic degrees. Certification programs are issued independently and are under progressive development toward formal recognition and accreditation within the Angolan higher education framework.

Contacting the team

For questions about the initiative or the waitlist, use the contact page. We read submissions in order of receipt and do not guarantee individual responses during pre-launch development.
