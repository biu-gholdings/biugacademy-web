About BIU.G Academy

Practical education built for real life in Angola.

BIU.G Academy is a new way to learn practical skills in Angola — focused on real-life outcomes, not theory.

We are building programs that help people manage money, start businesses, and develop skills that improve income and opportunities.

What we are building

Initial programs focus on financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and digital skills designed for Angola's real economy.

Important note

Programs are not yet formally accredited, but are being developed in alignment with Angola's education standards.
