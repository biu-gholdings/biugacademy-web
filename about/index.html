About BIU.G Academy

BIU.G Academy is an Angola-first education initiative building practical learning for financial literacy, small business, digital skills, and future technical work.

Our mission

We are building practical education for people who want to improve income, understand money, start or grow businesses, and prepare for digital opportunities. The focus is not theory. The focus is usable skills for real life.

Built under Biu-g Holdings LLC

BIU.G Academy is developed under Biu-g Holdings LLC as part of a broader Angola-first education and infrastructure strategy.

Why Angola-first

Built for local reality

Lessons are designed around how people actually live, work, learn, and build in Angola.

Offline-first by design

Learning experiences will prioritize low-bandwidth access and download-friendly materials where possible.

Practical before prestige

BIU.G Academy focuses first on useful skills, discipline, and outcomes before formal recognition.

What we are building

Financial literacy

Small business skills

Digital skills

Technical foundations

Future developer ecosystem

Local language and audio-first learning

Regulatory clarity

BIU.G Academy is not a university and does not currently issue accredited academic degrees. Learning tracks are being developed with attention to Angola's education standards, including MESCTI and INAAREES guidance. Any future formal recognition will be published clearly when obtained.
