Programs

Course catalog areas under design. Sequencing, prerequisites, and release dates will be published as each track reaches a teachable state.

Financial Literacy

Budgeting, debt awareness, savings behaviour, and small-business financial hygiene. Educational only; not personalised financial advice.

Entrepreneurship

Customer discovery, unit economics, operations basics, and documentation habits for early-stage ventures.

Software Development

Programming fundamentals, version control, testing concepts, and delivery practices suitable for technical careers.

Digital Infrastructure

Foundational literacy in networks, security awareness, cloud concepts, and reliability thinking for technical staff.

Trading & Markets Education

Market structure, risk, and analytical vocabulary. Not a brokerage; no promises of returns.

AI & Automation

Workflow automation, model limitations, data handling, and responsible deployment in organisational settings.

Civic Technology

Data in the public interest, service design for institutions, and transparency-oriented tooling at a conceptual level.

Agriculture & Local Business Systems

Record-keeping, simple logistics, and digital tools that support agribusiness and local supply chains.

Certificates and degrees

Completion credentials, where offered, are issued by BIU.G Academy and describe skills covered in the material. They are not accredited academic degrees and are not substitutes for programmes recognised by INAAREES until such time as explicit recognition is obtained and published.
